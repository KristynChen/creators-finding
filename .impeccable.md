## Design Context

### Users
- **Creators**: Looking for brand collaborations and high-end platform presence.
- **Brands/Vendors**: Looking for high-quality, relevant creators for marketing campaigns.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice & Tone**: High-end, Efficient, Modern, Design-driven.
- **3-Word Personality**: 高级 (Premium), 高效 (Efficient), 潮流 (Trendy).
- **Emotional Goals**: Confidence, Sophistication, Trust, Aesthetic Delight.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: High-end, fresh, non-cliché (高级、清新，又不落于俗套).
- **References**: 
  - [Farm Minerals](https://www.awwwards.com/sites/farm-minerals) (Clean, organic/modern balance)
  - [Product Sliding Animation](https://www.awwwards.com/market/product_69babc_7b4014b254263331) (Smooth browsing interaction)
  - [Agency of Love & Logic](https://www.awwwards.com/sites/the-agency-of-love-logic) (Comfortable but sophisticated colors)
  - [With Radiance](https://www.awwwards.com/sites/https-www-withradiance-com) (Color impact)
  - [Sutera](https://www.awwwards.com/sites/sutera) (High-end tech without "AI cliché")
  - [The Lookback](https://www.awwwards.com/sites/the-lookback) (Minimalist product layout)
  - [Royal Ontario Museum](https://www.awwwards.com/sites/royal-ontario-museum-rom) (Experimental/Premium typography & layout)
- **Anti-References**: 
  - NO "Generic AI" style (no glowing nodes, futuristic sci-fi blue/black grids).
  - NO "Standard Tech" style (no circuit boards, binary code).
- **Theme**: Premium and fresh. Avoid "too AI-ified" look.

### Design Principles
1. **Sophisticated Minimalism**: Use generous white space and clean typography to evoke a high-end feel.
2. **Impactful Color & Imagery**: Use color as a bold statement or subtle wash, avoiding generic gradients.
3. **Smooth, Intentional Motion**: Prioritize high-quality animations (like smooth sliding/scrolling) that feel premium and effortless.
4. **Non-Traditional Layouts**: Break away from standard bento boxes or generic landing page patterns where appropriate to feel more "design-forward".
5. **Fresh Clarity**: Ensure the interface feels "fresh" and easy to breathe, emphasizing efficiency through clarity.
